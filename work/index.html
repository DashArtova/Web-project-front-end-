DESIGNER


The Russian brand Loom Moscow showed a new lukbook, inspired by the 1970s.
According to the designer Svetlana Rodina, the starting point for the collection was the movie "Gray Gardens" about the cousin of Jacqueline Kennedy Edith Bouvier
Beale: the line included the delicate dresses of various colors, checkered
trousers, velvet bombarders and much more.

The book was photographed by photographer Antonina Zharko.

In 2016, Svetlana successfully graduated from the Moscow State Strocanov Academy, of industrial and applied arts, faculry of restoration and painting, which helps her not only to invent collections, but also to create model sketches for them.

The team of Loom Moscow cooperates with the best suppliers of natural fabrics, stricly controls the quality of planting and tailoring products at its own production facilities.
